Game Defaults

Starting HP by Player Count

  • 2 players: 50 HP
  • 3–4 players: 40 HP
  • 5–6 players: 60 HP

Turn Order

Players roll a D20 to determine turn order. Highest goes first. Ties are broken by rerolling only the tied players.

When enabled, dice roll automatically when the turn order screen appears.

When enabled, the game starts automatically after turn order is determined.

Gameplay

When enabled, dice roll automatically when each player's turn begins.

When enabled, damage from hits is automatically assigned to random opponents instead of choosing targets.

How to Play

The physical game uses two log rounds painted with D20 patterns—one for odd numbers (1–19), one for even (2–20). Players throw real knives at the targets based on what they roll!

Setup Select 2–6 players, choose unique names and colors, roll D20 for turn order (highest first; ties reroll).
Gameplay HP scales by count (2: 50, 3–4: 40, 5–6: 60). Roll 3 D20s, throw at matching targets, tap highlighted zones to claim hits, assign each hit’s full damage to one opponent (can’t split).
Special Rules Dropped knife = -1 HP. Eliminated at 0 HP. Last player standing wins!
